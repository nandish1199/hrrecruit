Sourcing Meaning

The process of Searching, Identifying, Contacting potential Candidates, After Sourcing there Comes Recruiting.

Recruitment Meaning

Recruitment refers to the process of Identifying, Attracting, Searching, Screening, Interviewing, Selecting and onboarding employees, it involves everything from the identification to onboarding.

HRM Meaning

HRM - Human Resource management
Managing people within an organization. Like improving Grievence Handeling, Co-ordination, Solving Conflicts, Training, Motivating Employees.

Head Hunting

Recruiting highely skilled Employees to the prominent jobs like Business Analyst, Software Architect, Management jobs etc.

Bulk/Volume Hiring

The process of hiring large no of skilled employees within a short period of time.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS

ANANYSING MAN POWER NEEDS

Here we analze the man power needs in the organisation, And we also analyze on which domain the man power is needed like Management, IT, Marketing e.t.c.

SEARCHING

Here we search for Qualified & Eligible candidates from the job portals like (Naukri, Monster, LinkedIn, Indeed) and also from the Campus interviews.

SCREENING

Here we filter candidates by Experience, Qualification and by Projects they have made And for the filtered candidate we assig the next interwiew round.

INTERVIEW

Here we check candidate's Interest, Skills, Fluency in Language,Expected CTC, Confidence and whether he/she is willing to relocate to the desired job location.

SELECTION & ONBOARDING

Here we train the candidates on Good ethics ,Rules & Regulations and About the Organization, And after this we select the candidates & Introduce them to the organisation.
