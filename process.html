TYPES OF RECRUITING

INTERNAL RECRUITING

Recruiting people from INSIDE the organization.

EXTERNAL RECRUITING

Recruiting people from OUTSIDE the organization.

RETAINED RECRUITING

Rrcruiting the retained people inside the organization.

EMPLOYEE REFERRALS

Recruiting people who are suggested by current employees.

EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Recruiting people from the colleges by campus interviews.

E-RECRUITMENT

Recruiting people from internet by using job portals and social media like (naukri, shine, monster, linked in

CONSULTANCY SERVICES

On boarding through consultancy service based companies, which will provide the eligible people to the organization.
